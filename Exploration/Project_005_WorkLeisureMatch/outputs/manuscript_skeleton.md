## Key Notation Used
- θ: worker type (Confucian norms, responsibility, family pressure)
- θ*: equilibrium threshold
- θ^{SP}: social planner threshold
- Φ* = 1 − F(θ*): equilibrium involution rate
- h ∈ {h_L, h_H}: work-hour regime
- ℓ_h = T − h: leisure under regime h
- L̄(θ*) = ℓ_L − Φ*·Δℓ: aggregate leisure
- μ(ℓ_i, L̄): Poisson meeting rate
- T_match = 1/μ: expected time to first match
- n_i = max{0, (T̄ − T_match)/τ}: expected fertility
- γ: rat-race intensity
- π(γ) = W_H − W_L: overtime wage premium
- τ*: optimal corrective overtime tax
