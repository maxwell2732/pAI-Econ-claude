**Verdict explanation:**
All seven checks pass. The empirical context for this paper is unusually well-supported: China's VBP program is large-scale, well-documented, and the key institutional features (price reduction magnitude, insurance coverage, bioequivalence-based substitution, and originator exit) are all confirmed by multiple independent sources. The paper can present China's VBP as a real-world motivating context, not merely as a stylized hypothetical, provided the acknowledged simplifications (uniform λ, homogeneous patient population) are clearly labeled as tractability assumptions in the model section.

**Required action before proceeding:**
None — proceed to Stage 3 (Theory Persona Council). The paper's introduction may describe China's VBP as the motivating empirical context. The model section must clearly state that treating λ as uniform is a tractability simplification, and the conclusion may discuss λ heterogeneity as an avenue for extension.
